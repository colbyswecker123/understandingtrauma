Send hope forward.

Write something gentle, steady, and encouraging for someone who may need it today.

A good starting point is: “Just in case nobody told you today…”

Helpful message style

  • Keep it short, gentle, and kind.
  • Avoid graphic details or triggering language.
  • Write like you are speaking to one person.
  • Offer hope without minimizing pain.
